## Param Overview

Param is Turkey's first licensed e-money institution, serving 10M+ users and processing $10B+ annually across its embedded finance ecosystem. It offers card issuing, digital wallets, POS, BNPL, and open banking solutions for both consumers and businesses.

## My Role

I lead growth initiatives at Param, Turkey's leading embedded finance platform, driving user acquisition, engagement, and revenue through product-led strategies.

I work at the intersection of product and marketing, launching features, optimizing conversion funnels, and scaling adoption across digital channels. I define KPIs, set up dashboards, and run conversion analyses to inform decisions across acquisition and retention.
