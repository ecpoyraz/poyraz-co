## Peerbie Overview

Peerbie is an ai powered workOS that blends project management, internal communication, and AI workflows into a unified platform. Designed for fast-moving teams, Peerbie combines chat, tasks, documents, and automation, eliminating tool overload. With customers across 15+ countries, Peerbie is used by startups and enterprises to simplify operations and enhance productivity.

### Overview of My Role

As a Growth Consultant, I collaborated directly with the CEO and marketing team to reshape Peerbie's product vision, execute a successful AI relaunch, and drive measurable growth in users and revenue.

![Peerbie platform overview](/images/peerbie-1.png)

### Product Pivot to AI

I initiated a strategic review of Peerbie's existing capabilities and identified opportunities to embed AI, like project creation with AI, digital twin and smart reports. Worked with product and design teams to integrate these features into the core experience.

I also redefined the product narrative to position Peerbie as an intelligent work assistant, not just a task tool.

![Peerbie AI features](/images/peerbie-2.png)

### Launching Peerbie AI

I created and executed the launch strategy for the new AI version, including messaging frameworks, landing page copy, in-app onboarding content, and email sequences.

I led outreach across key channels to attract early adopters, shaped the product's positioning for traction on platforms like LinkedIn and Product Hunt, and helped coordinate launch timelines with the dev team.

![Peerbie AI launch](/images/peerbie-3.png)

### Marketing & Sales Automations

Built automated outbound and onboarding flows to support Peerbie's AI relaunch. Using tools like Apollo, Lemlist, and Intercom, we launched targeted email sequences and lifecycle journeys that improved reply rates and boosted activation.

I also implemented lead scoring and CRM syncs to streamline demo bookings, and designed a high-converting landing page with full analytics tracking. These systems helped increase user retention and scale qualified lead generation without adding manual workload.

![Peerbie marketing automations](/images/peerbie-4.png)

### Growth & Revenue

Designed and launched outbound campaigns, lifecycle flows, and content updates to drive qualified traffic and conversions.

Leveraged no-code tools and CRM data to create segmented journeys that improved onboarding and user retention. Optimized key conversion points and provided analytics insights to iterate quickly post-launch.
