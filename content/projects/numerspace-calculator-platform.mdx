## NumerSpace Overview

NumerSpace is a bilingual calculator platform with 90+ free online tools across 15 categories, spanning finance, health, education, work, and daily life. From loan, inflation, and salary to BMI, VAT, and more, it brings the calculations people search for every day into one fast, free place, in both Turkish and English.

Unlike the other projects here, NumerSpace is my own product. I did not market someone else's company, I built and shipped this one myself.

### Overview of My Role

I am the founder and builder. I conceived the product, designed it, and wrote it end to end on a modern AI-assisted stack, Next.js, React, TypeScript, Tailwind, and Sanity. It is the project where I get to be the marketer and the maker at the same time, turning a growth thesis into a real, shipped product.

### Built for Organic Search

NumerSpace is a programmatic SEO play at its core. Every tool is a server-rendered page with structured data, both SoftwareApplication and FAQPage JSON-LD schemas, per-tool metadata, and bilingual URLs rewritten at the edge so Turkish and English both rank. The goal is simple: capture the high-intent searches people already make, loan calculator, BMI, salary, VAT, inflation, and answer them instantly.

![NumerSpace loan calculator](/images/numerspace-loan2.webp)

### One Space, Many Tools

The platform spans 90+ calculators across 15 categories, each built on the same clean architecture: pure typed math, a focused interactive component, and an SEO-ready page. A command palette, favorites, and recent tools tie it together, so the breadth never gets in the way of finding the one tool you need.

### Product and Design

Every tool is clean, fast, and free, with instant results and no clutter. I built a custom design system with its own typography and orange brand, light and dark modes, and an accessible calculator experience, sliders, tabs, and full result breakdowns, so each tool feels considered rather than generic.

### The Bigger Picture

NumerSpace is a production, bilingual platform of 90+ tools across 15 categories, taken from idea to live product end to end. It is the clearest proof that I do not just market products, I can envision, build, and grow them myself.
