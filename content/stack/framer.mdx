## How I use it

Framer is my most loved web builder currently, which evolved from a UI and prototyping tool in 2020 into a nocode web builder in 2022. It has everything you will need to ship wonderful websites fast: CMS, custom design inside a canvas like in Figma, crazy good performance thanks to react and server-side rendering, advanced motion capabilities due to their own animation library Framer Motion and a growing amount of useful third-party integrations.
